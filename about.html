ABOUT US

Major RB Properties

Major RB Properties is a property development company dedicated to creating accessible land ownership opportunities in Moshate Gardens, Soshanguve. We design and develop land projects that balance convenience, growth potential, and long-term value.

Who we are

Our mission is to make land ownership not just a purchase, but a pathway to sustainable living, financial stability, and generational wealth.

We focus on land as an investment and a foundation for personal and family growth, ensuring that every development provides both immediate accessibility and long-term potential.

Our Journey

2020 • Foundation

Established with a vision to make land ownership accessible to everyone in Soshanguve.

2021 • Phases 1 & 2

Successfully launched our first developments, providing over 400 plots for local families.

2023 • Infrastructure Expansion

Implemented full water and electricity infrastructure across all active phases.

2025 • Moshate Gardens

Current development of premium residential stands in the heart of Moshate Gardens.
